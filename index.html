Leonardo F. R. Ribeiro

(Leonardo Filipe Rodrigues Ribeiro)

I'm a 3rd year computer science PhD student at UKP Lab and AIPHES at Technische Universität Darmstadt, Germany. My supervisor is Prof. Iryna Gurevych.

I received a BS degree in Computer Engineering from the Centro Federal de Educação Tecnológica de Minas Gerais (CEFET-MG), in 2014. I studied for one year at the Universidad del País Vasco, Spain, attending classes from September 2012 until June 2013. I, then, received a Master degree in Computer and Systems Engineering from the Universidade Federal do Rio de Janeiro (COPPE/PESC/UFRJ), in 2017. My advisor was Prof. Daniel Figueiredo.

I'm broadly interested in the intersection of deep learning (graph representation learning and graph neural networks) and natural language processing (NLP). My current research focuses are on graph representation learning for natural language processing and text generation from graph-based data.
