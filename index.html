Leonardo Filipe Rodrigues Ribeiro
(Leo Ribeiro)

Computer Engineer

Short Bio

Leonardo Ribeiro is currently a first year computer science PhD student at UKP Lab and AIPHES at Technische Universität Darmstadt, Germany.

Leonardo was born in Ipatinga, Minas Gerais, Brazil. He is a computer enginner living in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.

He received a BS degree in Computer Engineering from the Federal Center for Technological Education of Minas Gerais (CEFET-MG), in 2014. He studied for one year at the University of Basque Country, Spain, attending classes from September 2012 until June 2013.

He, then, received a Master degree in Computer and Systems Engineering from the Federal University of Rio de Janeiro (COPPE/PESC/UFRJ), in 2017. His advisor was the Professor Daniel Ratton Figueiredo.
