Leonardo Ribeiro

Short Bio

Leonardo Ribeiro is currently a first year computer science PhD student at UKP Lab and AIPHES at Technische Universität Darmstadt, Germany. His supervisor is Prof. Iryna Gurevych.

Leonardo was born and and grew up in Ipatinga, Minas Gerais in Brazil. He received a BS degree in Computer Engineering from the Federal Center for Technological Education of Minas Gerais (CEFET-MG), in 2014. He studied for one year at the University of Basque Country, Spain, attending classes from September 2012 until June 2013.

He, then, received a Master degree in Computer and Systems Engineering from the Federal University of Rio de Janeiro (COPPE/PESC/UFRJ), in 2017. His advisor was Prof. Daniel Figueiredo.
