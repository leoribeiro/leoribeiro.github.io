Leonardo F. R. Ribeiro

(Leonardo Filipe Rodrigues Ribeiro)

Leonardo Ribeiro is a second year computer science PhD student at UKP Lab and AIPHES at Technische Universität Darmstadt, Germany. His supervisor is Prof. Iryna Gurevych.

He received a BS degree in Computer Engineering from the Federal Center for Technological Education of Minas Gerais (CEFET-MG), in 2014. He studied for one year at the University of Basque Country, Spain, attending classes from September 2012 until June 2013. He, then, received a Master degree in Computer and Systems Engineering from the Federal University of Rio de Janeiro (COPPE/PESC/UFRJ), in 2017. His advisor was Prof. Daniel Figueiredo.

His current research focuses are on Neural Graph Methods for Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Text Generation from graph-based data.
